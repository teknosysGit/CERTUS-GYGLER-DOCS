??? danger "Hizard: **Danger to life from handling toxic or highly reactive substances (storage, use, mixing, and disposal)**"

    ![Handling Toxic](../_shared_assets/icon_handling_toxic.png){ .img-icon width="80px" height="80px"}
    
    ► Workstations should be properly equipped with additional ventilation, gloves, goggles, protective clothing, and protective headgear
    
    ► Wear personal protective equipment specified in the manufacturer's safety data sheet
    
    ► Observe storage containers, ambient conditions (temperatures, air humidity), and premises specified in the manufacturer's safety data sheet and other regulations
    
    ► Avoid contact with other substances (can be lethal). Follow and implement the manufacturer's instructions
    
    ► Do not inhale vapors of toxic or highly reactive substances (can be lethal)
    
    ► Avoid every direct skin contact with a toxic or highly reactive medium (can be lethal)
    
    ► Avoid oral intake of any toxic medium (can be lethal)
    
    ► Avoid inappropriate protective equipment that can lead to skin contact or ingestion of a toxic medium (can be lethal)
    
    ► In case of skin contact or ingestion of any toxic substance despite safety precautions taken, immediately seek a doctor's consultation
    
    ► Observe all requirements and regulations regarding the disposal of a medium, as well as objects and fluids that have come into contact with it (improper disposal can be lethal)
